About Us

The IC Design Club, a part of NIE-AICTE Idealab, aims to provide practical knowledge in VLSI and IC design. Our mission is to prepare students for a career in the semiconductor industry by equipping them with cutting-edge skills and industry knowledge.

Our Activities

Our Activities

Workshops

We conduct hands-on workshops to dive deep into IC design concepts, tools, and technologies.

Projects

Innovative projects in VLSI, targeting real-world applications in semiconductor design.

Guest Lectures

Insights from industry experts on the latest trends in the semiconductor industry.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to create a learning platform where every member gets an opportunity to acquire & develop the skills in the semiconductor domain for pursuing a career in this field as well as for life-long learning.
